# $\color{yellow}{\textsf{RocketGoal.io Mods}}$
Mods for RocketGoal.io! Featuring advanced nickname changer, ping/FPS HUD, replay saving capabilities, account manager, and an easy stats dashboard.

#### $\color{blue}{\textsf{How To Install:}}$
Open the code section of the repository. Click "Code", then click "Download ZIP".

Then go to <chrome://extensions/>, turn on Developer Mode, and press "Load Unpacked".
<img width="1365" height="597" alt="Screenshot 2026-06-10 11 01 21 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/53eb7ab8-d216-4a88-b3d0-a8cab4a8a75d" />
It will open up your files app. Right click on the ZIP file called RocketGoal.io-Mods and press "Extract All".
Close the window that pops up when you extract the ZIP.
Double-click on the folder with the same name. It should open to have another folder with that name. select it and click "Open".
It should load to your extensions, and it's ready to use.

#### $\color{blue}{\textsf{How To Use:}}$
###### Nickname Changer
Click the puzzle piece in the top right corner.
Navigate to the extension called RocketGoal.io Tools, then click on it.
It will open the nickname changer.
As you type text into the box, the preview box will automatically update with how it will look in-game. Use the toolbar to change formatting and even save your favorite nicknames and styles for easy use. When done, click Update and Reload to save your name.
###### Ping/FPS HUD
When you open an online game, the ping/FPS HUD will update with your ping and frames per second four times a second. It appears in the top right corner of your screen. It measures ping to the RocketGoal.io server.
###### Replay Saver
This feature was inspired by the replay system in Rocket League. When you complete a match, a prompt will appear in the top right corner of your screen. To save your replay, press "Yes, save!". If you don't want to, click "Dismiss". If you save it, the extension will download a .webm file of your perspective during the game.
###### Account Manager
Do you have two accounts? Use this tool to sign in back and forth easily between them. On the main menu, the button is on the bottom middle of the screen. Click it and you will see a menu that lets you save accounts and sign in to them without having to go through the normal sign-in process.
###### Stats Dashboard
This tool is next to the accounts tool on the main menu. It will display your ranks, your records, a simplified version of your name, and more. Use this tool to keep track of your progression in-game.
